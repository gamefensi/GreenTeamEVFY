The EVFY Sustainability Roadmap

Our Sustainability Index is a program that provides green conscious members the platform to make informed carbon reduction decisions, with levels of services that consumers are comfortable with.

As part of the program, EVFY offers consumers subscription tiers and a roadmap to make the most informed decisions. This includes monthly reports, access to green products and services and offers a forum for members to share green lifestyle tips and a community to help others to become more ‘green.'
